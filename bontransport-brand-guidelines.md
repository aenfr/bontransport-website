# Bontransport S.L. — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Story

### Origin
Bontransport is a family freight company rooted in the roads of Spain since the 1960s. What began as one man — Salvador Gomez Cucarella — with a single truck running national routes, became a cross-European logistics operation spanning three generations. Formalized as Bontransport S.L. in 2008 by his son Oscar Gomez Jimenez, the company carries forward six decades of road knowledge, client trust, and a deeply personal commitment to every shipment.

### Mission
To deliver every cargo on time, in perfect condition, anywhere in Europe — with the care and accountability of a family business.

### Vision
To be the most trusted freight partner for businesses across Europe — where technology meets family values, and every client is treated like the first.

### Core Values
1. **Puntualidad** — Time is a promise. We keep it.
2. **Seguridad** — Your cargo is protected by insurance, technology, and our reputation.
3. **Compromiso** — We treat your needs as our own. Always have, since 1960.
4. **Transparencia** — Real-time GPS tracking. No guesswork. Full visibility.

## Logo Direction

### Concept
The logo should combine two ideas: **forward motion** (a road, arrow, or truck silhouette moving right) and **heritage** (the "B" initial as a strong, trustworthy anchor). The "bon" in Bontransport means "good" — the mark should feel solid, dependable, and premium.

### Primary Logo
- **Wordmark:** "BONTRANSPORT" in Montserrat Bold, with "BON" in Signal Orange (`#E85D26`) and "TRANSPORT" in Deep Navy (`#0B2545`)
- **Logomark:** A stylized "B" with an integrated forward arrow or road motif
- **Descriptor:** Small text below: "S.L. — Transporte Internacional" in Source Sans Pro

### Logo Versions
1. **Full lockup:** Logomark + Wordmark + Descriptor (website header, documents)
2. **Horizontal:** Logomark left + Wordmark right (email signatures, invoices)
3. **Compact:** Logomark only (favicon, app icon, truck branding)
4. **Mono white:** For dark backgrounds (truck livery, dark sections)
5. **Mono dark:** For light backgrounds (letterhead, contracts)

### Clear Space
Minimum clear space = height of the "B" in the logomark on all sides.

### Logo Don'ts
- Never stretch or skew
- Never use colors outside the brand palette
- Never place on busy photographic backgrounds without a solid overlay
- Never add drop shadows, bevels, or gradients
- Never rotate

## Brand Voice

### Personality
Bontransport speaks like a **trusted family business partner** — direct, warm, confident, and professional. Not corporate jargon. Not casual slang. The voice of someone who has been doing this for 60 years and takes genuine pride in their work.

### Tone Positioning
```
Professional ●●●●○ (not stiff)
Warm         ●●●●○ (not overly familiar)
Confident    ●●●●● (earned through decades)
Direct       ●●●●● (no filler, no fluff)
Technical    ●●○○○ (plain language first)
```

### Voice Principles

| Principle | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|
| **Be direct** | "Your cargo arrives on time. Every time." | "We endeavor to facilitate timely logistical outcomes." |
| **Show heritage** | "Three generations on the road" | "Founded a while ago" |
| **Be human** | "Call Oscar directly — he'll sort it out" | "Contact our department for assistance" |
| **Be specific** | "GPS tracking you can access 24/7" | "Advanced technology solutions" |
| **Be confident** | "We've done this for 60 years" | "We hope to meet your expectations" |

### Language Notes
- Primary language: **Spanish** (Castilian)
- Secondary: English, French (per their flag toggles)
- Always use proper accents: mercancias -> mercancias, vehiculos -> vehiculos
- Avoid anglicisms when Spanish terms exist
- Use "usted" for formal client-facing copy, "tu" for social media

## Visual Identity System

### Photography Style
- **Subject:** Trucks on European roads, warehouses, cargo being loaded, scenic highway shots
- **Mood:** Dawn/dusk golden hour lighting. Conveys motion and reliability.
- **Color treatment:** Slightly warm, high contrast, desaturated backgrounds with pops of orange
- **Avoid:** Stock-looking photos with fake smiles, overly staged scenes
- **Prefer:** Real fleet photos, real team, real routes. Authenticity over polish.

### Iconography
- **Style:** Line icons with 2px stroke, rounded caps
- **Color:** Deep Navy (`#0B2545`) on light backgrounds, White on dark
- **Set:** GPS pin, truck, clock, shield, warehouse, pallet, route/map, phone, email
- **Corner radius:** Consistent 2px rounds on all icons

### Patterns & Textures
- **Primary pattern:** Subtle diagonal lines at 45 degrees (evokes road markings)
- **Usage:** Section dividers, card backgrounds, hero overlays at 5-10% opacity
- **Map motif:** Faded European route map as a background texture for hero sections
